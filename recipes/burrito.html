Burrito

Description

A burrito is a Mexican dish consisting of a flour tortilla wrapped around a savory filling, typically including ingredients like rice, beans, meat (such as beef, chicken, or pork), cheese, and various toppings like salsa, sour cream, and guacamole. The tortilla is usually folded to fully enclose the filling, making it easy to eat by hand. Burritos can be customized with a variety of flavors and ingredients, making them a popular and versatile meal.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • White rice
  • Black beans
  • Chicken
  • Guacamole
  • Tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Warm up your tortilla
  2. Scoop rice onto tortilla
  3. Scoop black beens on top of rice
  4. Scoop chicken on top
  5. Scoop guacamole on top
  6. Small scoop of chopped tomatoes on top
  7. Small scoop of sour cream on top
  8. Sprinkle of lettuce on top
  9. Wrap it up like they do at Chipotle
  10. Enjoy your meal!
